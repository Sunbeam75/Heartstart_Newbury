Venue

50% of all our received donations are given to the British Heart Foundation

Contact us: office@heartstartnewbury.org.uk

We ask that you provide a spacious venue free of charge for the course. If this is not possible then we can book a venue for you, though please be aware that this may incur costs you need to meet. We will advise you of any such costs before any bookings are made. We will travel to any venue (or home) in Newbury Berkshire or within the (reasonable) surrounding area. 


We provide dates suitable to your convenience. Please email us.




Cost

The course is provided FREE of charge.


We do accept donations for our ongoing work. To this end we will always provide you with a suggested donation figure (based on the size of your organisation, and the number of people attending a course) when you book. Though please understand this is not expected as it is only a suggested donation because the course is provided FREE.

We want you to book!

Thank you for your generosity.
